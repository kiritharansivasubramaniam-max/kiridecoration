About Kiri Decoration

Kiri Decoration is a creative wedding décor company built on passion, culture, and the belief that every celebration deserves its own unique beauty. What started as a small dream has now grown into a trusted name for elegant, thoughtful, and emotionally rich event styling. At Kiri Decoration, we understand that a wedding is much more than an event — it is a moment of connection, tradition, and new beginnings. Our mission is to transform these special moments into unforgettable visual experiences through décor that reflects love, personality, and cultural identity. We specialize in designing and styling everything from traditional Tamil weddings to modern luxury ceremonies. From floral mandaps, stage décor, and lighting design to reception themes, home events, and engagement setups, every element we create is crafted with precision and meaning. Our designs blend timeless charm with contemporary aesthetics, offering couples a perfect balance of tradition and elegance. What truly sets Kiri Decoration apart is our deep respect for cultural heritage. Whether it is incorporating Tamil Sri Lankan elements, honoring traditional customs, or designing modern, luxurious themes, our team ensures that every detail carries emotion and authenticity. We believe décor should not just look beautiful — it should carry the warmth and identity of the people we design for. Every event we take on is approached with care, creativity, and genuine dedication. From the first consultation to the final setup, we work closely with each couple to understand their vision, their story, and the atmosphere they want to create. Our goal is to make the planning process smooth, enjoyable, and stress-free, while delivering décor that exceeds expectations. Over time, Kiri Decoration has become known for its artistic touch, unique concepts, and ability to turn ordinary spaces into breathtaking backdrops. Our designs have helped countless couples celebrate their love in settings that feel magical, personal, and truly unforgettable. At Kiri Decoration, we don’t just decorate — we bring dreams to life, shape memories, and create moments that stay in the heart forever.

Our approach

We focus on personalized consultations, mood boards, and careful execution so you can enjoy your day while we manage the details.

Meet the Founder

Kiritharan — The heart behind Kiri Decoration is Kiritharan, a proud Sri Lankan Tamil whose artistic roots lie in the cultural city of Jaffna. Once a profound and expressive artist, he brought his creativity and passion to Chennai, where he now designs beautiful wedding décor for couples who want something truly special. His work reflects the richness of Sri Lankan Tamil tradition, the warmth of his heritage, and the elegance of modern wedding aesthetics.
